Declaration of Consent

By clicking I Agree, effective as of the date of registration of the user in the Parrot application, both the Investigators- Data Receiver, one who receives the shared data (images) and the Data Releaser, one who shares the data hereby agree to its terms specified below:

Rights and Responsibilities of the Authorized Investigator(s):

  • Authorized Investigator(s) are affiliated with IIITD and the institute maintains an ethics or Institutional Review Board that reviews and approves research involving human subjects.
  • Authorized Investigator(s) are eligible to conduct independent research on the data shared by the Releaser.
  • Authorized Investigator(s) have current research ethics training addressing human subjects policy and issues that meet the Institution’s standards.
  • Authorized Investigator(s) agree to allow the use of the data exclusively for the purpose of scientific research or education under an academic, research, and government for non-commercial purposes. The shared data (images) may be used as illustrations in printed materials such as research reports, posters, and/or conference/seminar presentations. The shared data (images) will not be used in any way that would demean the Participant/Releaser.
  • Authorized Investigator(s) ensure that Releaser’s wishes about sharing their data are respected.
  • Authorized Investigator(s) ensure that the identity (name) of the participant will not be linked with the shared data (images).

I have read and understood the rights and responsibilities of the Authorized Investigator(s) and consent to the use of my shared information (images) for the purpose mentioned under the rights and responsibilities of Authorized Investigator(s).
